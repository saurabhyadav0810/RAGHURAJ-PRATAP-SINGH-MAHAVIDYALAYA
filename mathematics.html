B.Sc. in Mathematics

Duration: 6 Semesters (3 Year)

Bachelor of Science in Mathematics is designed to provide students with a broad understanding of mathematics and its applications. It focuses on the theory and techniques of mathematical analysis, helping students develop problem-solving and logical reasoning skills.

Overview

This program offers a comprehensive study of various branches of mathematics such as calculus, algebra, geometry, statistics, tensor, operation research, and more. Students will be trained in logical thinking and rigorous argument and learn how to formulate, analyze, and solve mathematical problems. The curriculum also encourages the use of mathematical software for problem-solving and modeling.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can find opportunities in sectors such as academia, finance, IT, data analysis, Data Analysis, Quantitative Analyst, computer programmer, Financial planner, Purchasing Manager, Chartered accountant, Insurance Underwriter, Investment banking, and government. Potential roles include Mathematician, Data Analyst, Actuary, Statistician, and Financial Analyst.

Program Learning Outcomes

  • Gain a comprehensive understanding of the key concepts, theories, and principles of mathematics.
  • Develop strong problem-solving skills and the ability to use logical reasoning to solve complex problems.
  • Acquire the ability to use mathematical software for problem-solving and modeling.

Labs

Our Mathematics Lab is a vibrant and dynamic space where students engage in hands-on mathematical exploration, problem-solving, operation research, Numerical analysis, MATLAB, SCILAB, C++, and innovation.

Facilities

The lab is equipped with a wide array of mathematical tools, manipulatives, and software that facilitate interactive and experiential learning. From geometric models and algebraic manipulation to graphing calculators and mathematical software, our lab provides students with the resources they need to visualize and comprehend abstract mathematical concepts.

Research Opportunities

The lab encourages students to engage in mathematical research projects, whether independently or in collaboration with faculty members. This fosters analytical thinking and creativity, allowing students to contribute to the advancement of mathematical knowledge.

Safety

While mathematical exploration may not involve physical risks, we prioritize creating a comfortable and inclusive learning environment where all students can engage with mathematical ideas without fear of judgment or intimidation. Our lab is a safe space for students to experiment, make mistakes, and learn from each other.

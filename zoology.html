B.Sc. in Zoology

Duration: 6 Semesters (3 Year)

The Bachelor of Science in Zoology is a three-year undergraduate program that entails advanced study and research in the fields of Biological, Oceanography, Comparative Physiology, Ecology, Developmental Biology, Cell Biology, Instrumentation, Vertebrate & Invertebrate Zoology, Parasitology, Environmental Biology and Toxicology etc., an in-depth program that explores the biology of animals, from cellular and molecular levels to entire ecosystems. It provides a comprehensive understanding of animal behavior, evolution, and environment.

Overview

This program covers a wide range of topics including animal physiology, genetics, ecology, evolution, and conservation. Students will study the diversity of animal life, understand the mechanisms of animal body functioning, and explore the role of animals in their ecosystems. Fieldwork and laboratory work are integral parts of the program, offering students hands-on experience in research and data collection. The program also emphasizes the development of analytical and problem-solving skills.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can find opportunities in fields such as wildlife conservation, research, academia, environmental consultancy, and the pharmaceutical industry. They can work as Zoologists, wildlife technicians, marine biologists, veterinary technicians, animal nutritionists, forest officers, veterinarians, research scientists, ecologists, scientific-technical writers, veterinary surgeons, wildlife biologists, environmental consultants, research scientists, and more.

Labs

Our defence Lab is a hub of defence operations and exploration, offering students the opportunity to immerse themselves in the captivating world of defence systems (map, globe, compass, etc.) and their interactions.

Facilities

Our lab is equipped with cutting-edge facilities that support a wide range of defence studies. From advanced defence systems equipment and culture media, our lab provides the tools necessary for in-depth investigations of national security and strategic studies.

Research Opportunities

We encourage students to engage in research projects that delve into exciting areas of national security with faculty members. Students can explore topics such as Military Officers, Defense Analysts, Security Managers, civilization safety and security, socioeconomic specialists, etc., contributing to the advancement of scientific knowledge. There are a lot of career opportunities in the field of military science.

Safety Measures

Safety is our top priority, and we strictly adhere to safety protocols to ensure the well-being of all lab participants. Students are provided with appropriate safety gear, including lab coats and safety goggles, to create a secure learning environment. Experienced lab staff and faculty members closely supervise all experiments to maintain a safe and controlled environment. The co-existence of a trained harmonious environment and spokespersons maintains safety and prevents contamination.

B.Sc. in Physics and Electronics

Duration: 6 Semesters (3 Year)

The Bachelor of Science in Physics and Electronics program offers an in-depth understanding of the fundamental principles of physics and electronics. It is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to explore the natural phenomena happening in the universe.

Overview

The program covers a broad spectrum of topics, including Classical Mechanics, Solid State Physics, Modern Physics, Spectroscopy, Thermal Physics, Quantum Mechanics, Theory of Relativity, Electromagnetism, Electronics, and Communication Systems. It also introduces students to cutting-edge areas of research in Advanced Physics, Condensed Matter Physics, and Materials Science. Students will gain their practical knowledge in laboratories where they will conduct experiments by using modern instrumentation. The curriculum encourages analytical thinking and problem-solving skills, and it forms a strong foundation for further study and research in the field.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can find opportunities in broad sectors like academia, research institutions, engineering, information technology, data analysis, and space science. They can work as Physicists, Research Scientists, Data Analysts, and many more roles.

Program Learning Outcomes

  • It provides a platform to understand the fundamental principles as well as theories of physics and electronics, which one can apply in solving complex problems.
  • Practical skills developed in laboratory work can be utilized in modern instrumentation and data analysis techniques.

Labs

The physics laboratory is a center for exploring scientific knowledge in various areas of research and discovery. It also provides a platform for the students with hands-on experiences to extend their understanding of the fundamental principles of physics.

Facilities

The lab is well-equipped with modern apparatus and instruments that facilitate a wide range of experiments across various branches of physics, including mechanics, electromagnetism, optics, thermodynamics, modern physics, and electronics (Digital and Analog). Departmental facilities include state-of-the-art experimental setups and precision measuring instruments.

Research Opportunities

The Physics Lab offers a diverse array of experiments that align with the syllabus. We encourage our students to engage in research projects and improve their creativity and critical thinking skills in various areas. The lab provides a platform for students to connect with faculty members to discuss their problems and enable them to contribute to the frontiers of physics.

Safety Measures

Safety is our utmost priority, and the lab strictly adheres to safety guidelines and protocols. Students are provided with safety gear, such as lab coats and safety goggles, to ensure a secure learning environment. Trained lab assistants and faculty members closely supervise all experiments to maintain safety and prevent accidents.
